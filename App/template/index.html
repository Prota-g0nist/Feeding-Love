About Us

At Feeding With Love, we believe in the power of community and the importance of sharing resources. Our mission is to connect food donors with local food banks to ensure that no meal goes to waste and everyone has access to nutritious food.

Founded in 2023, our organization has been dedicated to fighting hunger and reducing food waste. We work with a network of food banks, donors, and volunteers who share our vision of a world where food is abundant and accessible to all. Through our platform, we make it easy for individuals and businesses to donate surplus food, and for food banks to efficiently distribute these donations to those in need.

We are passionate about creating sustainable solutions for hunger relief and are committed to fostering a culture of giving and gratitude. Our team is composed of dedicated professionals and volunteers who work tirelessly to make a positive impact in our communities.

Join us in our mission to give food and save lives. Together, we can make a difference and ensure that everyone has a seat at the table.
